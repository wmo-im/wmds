6-08 Description of the schedule of observation. Note: Schedules are defined in terms of months covered, weekdays covered, hours and minutes covered during each day. At present, schedules within the hour are not supported. A complete definition of a schedule requires specification of the temporalReportingInterval, and may require the specification of diurnalBaseTime. [Phase 1]<br/>
